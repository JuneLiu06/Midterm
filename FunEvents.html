Alpine Snowboarding Race
Dive into the thrilling world of Alpine Snowboarding Races. Watch as competitors zigzag down groomed runs, vying for the best time. Steep descents, sharp turns, and the quest for ultimate speed define this event. Perfect for those who love the adrenaline rush!
Slopestyle Showdown
Experience the artistry and daring of Slopestyle Snowboarding. Riders navigate a series of obstacles including rails, jumps, and other terrain park features. Points are awarded for amplitude, originality, and quality of tricks. A spectacle of style and skill!
Halfpipe Heroes
Join us in the snowboarder's playground – the Halfpipe! Riders perform tricks while going from one side of the halfpipe to the other, getting air on each side. Gravity-defying spins, flips, and grabs are the name of the game. A must-watch for true snowboarding enthusiasts!
Big Air Blast
Witness snowboarders taking to the skies in the Big Air event. Competitors ride down a set course leading to a jump, aiming to attain substantial height and distance, all while performing tricks. It's breathtaking, awe-inspiring, and not for the faint of heart!
Boardercross Battle
Brace yourself for Boardercross – where multiple riders race down a course studded with various obstacles including jumps, berms, and other hindrances. It's a race of speed, agility, and strategic maneuvering. Every second counts!
Freeride Frontier
Experience the freedom of Freeride Snowboarding. Riders choose their own line and path down off-trail, ungroomed areas of the mountain. It's all about natural terrain, creativity, and raw snowboarding skills. A treat for those who love uncharted terrains!
